Welcome to Let's Boost It!

If you're trying to improve your Internet connectivity performance, you're in the right place to be!

You reached the home of the Booster project.

We want to improve our network connectivity by finally having a plug-and-play solution that allows us to take full advantage of our mobile devices! We believe in the strength of collaboration, and we apply the very same concept to devices, this time with the focus on Internet connectivity.

We're building a new multi-homed router device, which will replace your current home router or extend it. The router will provide ports for connecting the mobile devices to it, and interfaces for connecting the devices that need to be.. boosted.

Among our early adopters and testers we have gamers, which thrive for a reliable access point which reserves a slice of the overall bandwidth available for a smooth gaming experience. We have professional photographers, which want to upload their pictures overnight and need a reliable link that will not let the upload interrupt, or last too long. We have also people that just want to watch Netflix together, splitting the data download consumption equally.

If you want to learn more, our forum is the perfect place to land to! You'll find technical and non-technical information.

We love Open Source Software, you'll find our company's project repository here!
